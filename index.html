Privacy Policy for MathFlash Guru

Last updated: February 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MathFlash Guru ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User-Provided Information

  • Username: A unique identifier chosen by you for game identification and leaderboard participation
  • Game Progress: Your advancement through various levels and challenges
  • Achievement Data: Records of completed achievements and milestones
  • High Scores: Your best performance records in different game modes

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Operating system version, device model, and unique device identifiers
  • Game Performance Data: Gameplay statistics, completion times, and accuracy rates
  • App Usage Data: Session duration, feature usage patterns, and interaction metrics
  • Technical Data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and debug information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Maintaining your game progress and achievements
  • Operating and updating leaderboards
  • Providing personalized gameplay experience
  • Saving your preferences and settings

3.2 App Improvement

  • Analyzing game performance and user behavior
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Optimizing game difficulty and progression
  • Developing new features and improvements

3.3 Communication

  • Sending critical updates about the App
  • Responding to your support requests
  • Notifying you about significant changes to this policy
